Frequently Asked Questions

Where is it?

99 Madison Ave, 12th floor
New York, NY 10016

Who's coming?

We expect 200 hackers from high schools all over the tri-state area to be attending StuyHacks.

Who can attend?

All students! Even though the hackathon is only for high schoolers, if you're in college or above, you can still apply to be one of our amazing mentors at this link!

Do I need experience?

No experience necessary. We will have plenty of mentors and resources. Come, learn, and experience your first hackathon at StuyHacks!

Are there any rules?

We want to ensure a positive experience for every participant. We encourage you to read our Code of Conduct.

What should I bring?

You should bring your laptop, phone, chargers, and sleeping bag. All food and other accommodations will be taken care of.

Can we form teams?

Of course you can! Teams must have at least two people and at most four people. There will be plenty of team building events on site and online.

Does it cost anything?

StuyHacks is 100% free. You won't have to spend a dime!

Will there be travel reimbursements?

Unfortunately, we will not be providing travel reimbursements this StuyHacks.
